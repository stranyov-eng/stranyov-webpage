Test Pattern Generator
Modes
LED
Checkerboard grid for LED panels. Set cell size in px, cols×rows with half-module support. Canvas sub-mode lets you composite multiple screens with individual X/Y positioning.
Projection
Multi-projector blending patterns. Choose projector resolution from presets, set cols×rows and blend %. Total resolution adjusts automatically. Canvas sub-mode for complex multi-projector layouts.
Dome
Square patterns for fulldome projection. Standard mode with grid, radial lines and safe area ring. Zones mode shows distance rings from center in %.
Features
Scroll wheel Zoom in/out centered on cursor position
Middle mouse Hold to pan around the canvas
Double click Reset zoom and pan to fit view
Math in fields Type expressions like 1920+200 or 3840/2 in any number input — press Enter or click away to apply
Mask (LED) Paint mode: left click to black out cells, right click to erase. Mask covers grid, IDs and circle but not info text and border
Add to Canvas Snapshot current screen settings into Canvas mode. Each screen keeps its own colors, blend, mask. Position with X/Y fields
Auto-save All settings persist across page reloads via localStorage. Logo uploads are not saved — re-upload after refresh
Export Downloads as name_WxH.png at full canvas resolution — no scaling
